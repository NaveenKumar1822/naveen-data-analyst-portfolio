Supply Chain Analytics 01

Nassau Candy Logistics Intelligence

A production-grade, end-to-end analytics pipeline designed to diagnose and expose systemic delivery failures across a multi-factory confectionery supply chain — processing over 10,000 shipment records across 196 unique geographic routes.

Business Problem

Operational reports contained severe data integrity anomalies and inaccurate lead-time calculations, making it impossible to identify true delivery bottlenecks and carrier performance issues at scale.

Analytical Approach

Built a forensic data cleaning and decryption pipeline to restore data accuracy. Engineered a medallion-style analytics architecture and developed route-level performance intelligence to expose operational inefficiencies.

Key Insights

  • Reverse-engineered and corrected programmatic date offsets of up to 1,634 days
  • Analyzed 10,000+ shipment records across 196 geographic routes
  • Computed Route Efficiency Scores (RES) and lead-time volatility metrics
  • Built a Streamlit app for dynamic SLA monitoring and route analysis
  • Leveraged gold-layer Parquet architecture for scalable performance

Business Impact

Enabled accurate operational reporting and route-level visibility, allowing stakeholders to identify bottlenecks, monitor carrier performance, and improve fulfillment decision-making.

Product & Growth Analytics 02

NexaFlow SaaS Funnel Analytics

An end-to-end funnel intelligence project investigating user behavior across the SaaS lifecycle — from signup through activation, conversion, and churn — using a 10,000-user behavioral dataset.

Business Problem

Despite healthy acquisition numbers, only 20% of users converted into paid customers while platform churn remained significantly high — signaling critical friction points in the funnel.

Analytical Approach

Conducted advanced SQL segmentation and behavioral cohort analysis to identify friction points, improve monetization efficiency, and uncover the primary drivers of retention across the full user lifecycle.

Key Insights

  • Activation identified as the strongest predictor of long-term retention
  • Non-converted users churn at nearly twice the rate of paid users
  • Day 6–10 conversion window identified as the most critical retention lever
  • Structural churn risk discovered within paid acquisition channels
  • Built a 4-page Power BI dashboard with advanced DAX measures

Business Impact

Provided actionable recommendations for marketing budget allocation, funnel optimization, and customer retention strategy — directly addressing the 80% non-conversion gap.

Gaming Industry Analytics 03

Player Behavior & Retention Analytics

An exploratory data analysis project investigating player behavior patterns across thousands of game titles to understand lifecycle engagement, retention, and abandonment behavior using the RAWG API.

Business Problem

Game studios traditionally rely on ownership volume and sales metrics — which fail to accurately represent long-term player engagement and retention, leaving critical lifecycle gaps undiagnosed.

Analytical Approach

Collected and analyzed gaming data from the RAWG API, engineered custom lifecycle KPIs, and investigated player retention patterns using behavioral analytics — building a lifecycle-focused framework that challenges legacy success metrics.

Key Insights

  • Engineered custom metrics: Completion Rate, Drop Rate, Engagement Rate
  • Review scores influence acquisition — but not retention
  • Early-stage player abandonment identified as the dominant retention challenge
  • Traditional unit-sales success metrics proven insufficient for lifecycle analysis
  • Lifecycle-focused retention framework developed for studio decision-making

Business Impact

Provided strategic recommendations for onboarding optimization, retention improvement, and more effective allocation of development resources based on behavioral data rather than sales proxies.
